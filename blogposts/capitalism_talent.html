IMO, IOI & similar type of people have been competing in problem-solving for years now. if, to this equation, one adds teams, collaboration, amazing compensation and benefits, we should not be surprised why they choose this route. we are expecting a level of moral responsibility from people we wouldn't uphold ourselves.
